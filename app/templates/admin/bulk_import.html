qhsJSON Format

CoachPrash's standard format for questions, hints, and solutions. Supports MCQ, fill-in-blank, and free response problems with LaTeX math and images.

Features

  • Problem types: mcq, fill_in_blank, frq
  • LaTeX: Use \( ... \) for inline, \[ ... \] for display math
  • Images: Use <img data-bucket-key='images/subject/topic/file.png' alt='...' /> in question_html, hints, or solutions
  • Hints: Strings or objects with text and cost_points
  • Solutions: Strings or objects with text
  • Fill-in-blank: Multiple accepted answers separated by ||
  • Free response: No auto-grading; correct_answer stores model answer shown after submission
